How to use the Interactive FSVA

This online version of the FSVA allows you to view each of the indicators collected for the Atlas by district and sub-district over three periods for national FVSA (2005, 2009 and 2015) and two periods for provincial FSVA (2010 and 2015). Here are a few simple instructions on how to use this interactive tool:

  1. Click the Language menu to select the language (Bahasa Indonesia or English Versions)

  2. Click the Area menu to select the FSVA coverage (Indonesia, Jawa Timur, Papua, Nusa Tenggara Timur and Nusa Tenggara Barat).

  3. Navigate through the various indicators by clicking on the food security dimensions and climatic factors on the left side of the page. The headers labeled Food Availability, Food Access, Food Utilization, and Climatic and Environmental Factors will reveal indicators relating to that dimension of food security

  4. Hovering over a district on a map reveals three data periods for that district and the currently selected indicator

  5. By clicking on a district, you can view the district profile – a summary of all of the chronic food insecurity indicators over three data periods in a table view

  6. Clicking on Table View on the top right side of a map will display all district values for the currently selected indicator in 2015. The search box can be used to filter provinces or districts

  7. To download the data being displayed in on the current map and Table View, click Download CSV
